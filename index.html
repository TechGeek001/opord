OPORD Builder Version 0.1

This application is intended to help U.S. Army platoon leaders develop operations orders. It is not associated with any United States Government organization.

The orders created with this application are formatted based on the Ranger Handbook and best practices taught in the Infantry Basic Officer Leader Course.

By using this application, you assume all risk for the products created. This application is NOT secure, and should not be used with sensitive information. For testing purposes, a copy of your order and all additional products may be saved to a remote server.

About

My name is Andrew Zitter. I'm currently a 2LT with 1-505 PIR, 82nd Airborne Division. Although I'm an 11-series MOS, my college background is in web development, application security, and user experience (I build it, keep hackers out of it, and make it easy to use). I created this app because I noticed that there was a wide variety of products that my fellow LTs were creating, some of them more professional and complete than others. This program is meant to make better products - both OPORDs and graphics, which makes us look better in front of our higher commanders.
