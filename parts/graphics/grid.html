Grid Lines

Point and Adjacent Line

Use this option if you know the location of a grid line intersection on the image, as well as the grid line immediately above, below, left, or right of the intersection. This is useful if you are referencing existing graphics.

Two Points

Use this option if you know where two eight digit grids are located on the image. Note that using this method has a margin of error of 10 meters and will not produce perfect results.

Point and Distance

Use this option if you know where an eight digit grid is located on the image, as well as a straight-line distance (e.g. a road or trench line). Note that using this method has a margin of error of 10 meters and will not produce perfect results.
